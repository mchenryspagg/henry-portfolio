Salesforce CRM Implementation

Velociti Bikes

Salesforce Analyst / Consultant · Conam Technologies · Apr–May 2026

View live demo ↗ Try the Web-to-Lead form ↗ View presentation ↗

The brief

Velociti Bikes — a premium retail bicycle shop specialising in high-end mountain bikes and professional repair — was experiencing a surge in demand from both walk-in traffic and online inquiries. Sales enquiries and repair tickets had grown beyond what manual record-keeping could handle. The mandate: move from fragmented, manual processes to a centralised digital system that captures every customer interaction, routes it intelligently, and surfaces operational health to leadership in real time.

Inside the build

Eleven discrete Salesforce capabilities — wired together so the bike shop runs from a single source of truth.

What I built

Integrated company website + customer intake portal

Designed and coded a fully integrated company website featuring a native customer intake portal — transitioning the client from fragmented, manual record-keeping to a central digital system.

Web-to-Lead automation

Engineered an automated Web-to-Lead configuration that funnels website contact inquiries straight into the Salesforce Lead object, triggering immediate validation and an automated verification message back to the customer.

Web-to-Case ticketing pipeline

Architected a custom Web-to-Case online booking form that captures real-time technical issues from the website directly into the Case object, establishing a standardised ticketing pipeline.

Background Lead Assignment Engine — round-robin

Built an advanced background Lead Assignment Engine utilising round-robin routing logic to automatically distribute inbound web prospects evenly to active sales queues — eliminating administrative delays and rep cherry-picking.

Record-triggered notification flows

Programmed customised, record-triggered notification flows that instantly fire structured email alerts containing live record links to the Sales Manager and assigned team members upon prospect intake.

Duplication rules — exact-match email

Configured multi-tier duplication rules utilising exact-match email variables to identify and flag potential duplicate records inside the Lead workspace layout, while allowing manual overrides for validated exceptions.

Data-guard validation rules

Implemented strict, data-guard validation rules at the UI level to preserve database quality — successfully blocking empty description inputs, restricting past-dated open opportunities, and freezing stage progressions unless contract values are explicitly entered.

Stage-triggered cross-functional automations

Developed complex, cross-functional record automations that listen to pipeline stages — automatically scheduling stage-specific checklist tasks (product demos, negotiation follow-ups) and dynamically creating live Asset records the moment a deal hits Closed Won.

Role-based data sharing model

Designed a secure, role-based data sharing model using Org-Wide Defaults (OWDs) and customised user profiles (Sales Representative, Service Agent, and Manager) to restrict commercial transaction metrics from support desks, and internal care logs from sales personnel.

Multi-object data analytics suite

Constructed a complete data analytics suite by building a matrix of multi-object summaries — including open opportunity value matrices by rep, case resolution durations, and open backlog age trackers.

Sales Pipeline, Service & Management Dashboards

Deployed an interactive, executive-ready Sales Pipeline Dashboard combining visual sales funnels, revenue forecasting charts, team workload leaderboards, active conversion gauges, and win/loss trend analytics. Built and launched a dedicated Service Dashboard and a high-level Management Dashboard to replace manual spreadsheet tracking with live visual telemetry on support desk response health, SLA compliance, and team performance.

Outcomes

  • ✓ Replaced fragmented, manual record-keeping with a centralised digital system spanning sales and service.
  • ✓ Eliminated administrative delays and rep cherry-picking via automated round-robin lead distribution.
  • ✓ Standardised the repair-ticket pipeline so every issue is captured, routed, and resolvable against an SLA.
  • ✓ Gave leadership live executive telemetry — pipeline health, service response, and SLA compliance — without spreadsheet overhead.
  • ✓ Locked down data quality at the UI layer (validation rules) and at the org layer (OWDs + profiles).

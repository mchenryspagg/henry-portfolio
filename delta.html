Salesforce · Data + AI + CRM

Delta Airlines

Preparing for AI as a Salesforce Administrator · Clicked · Oct–Dec 2024

Custom Salesforce solution for Delta Airlines combining Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Experience Cloud, and a Python loyalty-prediction model.

Project summary

Salesforce Administrator

  • · Designed custom objects — Frequent Fliers, Flights, and Bookings — aligned to the raw datasets for accurate mapping and integration.
  • · Imported and consolidated 9,000+ customer records via Data Import Wizard, triggers, and Batch Apex — resolving duplicate-entry challenges.
  • · Built a real-time customer interaction portal on Experience Cloud with a chatbot for support, flight updates, and frequent-flier engagement.
  • · Configured Service Cloud knowledge articles to enhance case deflection for upgrades and cancellations.

Data Analyst

  • · Cleaned and standardised 15,000+ entries in Python — handling missing values, inconsistent formats, and duplicates.
  • · Built a logistic regression model to predict customer loyalty using travel frequency and add-on spending; supported targeted loyalty programs and churn reduction.
  • · Identified a 50.2% flight-delay rate via predictive modelling and proposed data-driven optimisations.
  • · Surfaced behavior trends — top-flight routes, frequent-flier preferences, ancillary revenue opportunities — informing marketing strategy.
